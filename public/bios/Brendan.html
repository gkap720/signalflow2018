Brendan Glasson

Brendan Glasson (b. 1985, Providence, RI) is a composer and multimedia artist based in Oakland, CA, where he is an MFA candidate in the Electronic Music and Recording Media program at Mills College. Brendan has performed works at the Centre Pompidou-Metz, the MUDAM museum in Luxembourg, and the RISD museum in Providence, RI, and has held residencies at the Hewnoaks Artist Colony in Lovell, ME.
