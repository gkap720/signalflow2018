Anastasia Clarke

Anastasia Clarke (b. 1987) is an electronic music composer, sound designer, vocalist, performer, and interdisciplinary artist from New York. Conceiving performance as a modality for improving the quality of human experience, she is concerned with articulating the many healing and therapeutic efficacies of the live medium. Anastasia is currently a 2018 MFA candidate in Electronic Music and Recording Media at Mills College, where she has been conceiving musical instruments that call for visceral interaction between performers and interface.
