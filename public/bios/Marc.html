Marc Zollinger

Two years later, he moved with his family to Los Angeles where he developed an interest in Jazz. He studied classical guitar with Matthew Greif (Los Angeles Guitar Quartet), before moving to Boston to further pursue his musical studies at the Berklee College of Music.
